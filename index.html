Human-Centered Computing

Rooted in an interdisciplinary spirit, I explore the intersection of humanities, art, and science to design innovative, human-centered technologies. By combining creative thinking with technical rigor, I strive to develop meaningful solutions that bridge everyday life and advanced computing—expanding the frontiers of Human-Centered Computing.

AI-Driven Mixed Reality

I explore how the fusion of Artificial Intelligence and Mixed Reality can unlock new forms of interaction previously thought impossible. By combining AI’s adaptive intelligence with the immersive power of MR, I design human-centered systems that go beyond conventional boundaries—enabling intuitive, responsive, and meaningful experiences in both physical and digital spaces. Guided by a "Responsible Human-Aware" philosophy, my work aims to transform what technology can do for people.
